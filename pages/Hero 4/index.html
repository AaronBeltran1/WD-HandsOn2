Sanji

Powers

  • Diable Jambe
  • Sky Walk

Background

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, known for his incredible culinary skills and deadly fighting techniques. His dream is to find the All Blue, a legendary sea with fish from all over the world.
